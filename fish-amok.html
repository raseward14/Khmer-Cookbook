Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut the fish into bite sized pieces and set aside
  2. Clean and slice the vegetables
  3. In a large skillet, heat the curry sead oil, amok paste, shrimp paste, and coconut milk. Heat thoroughly, cooking until fragrant
  4. Add the vegetables, except kale, fish, and the rest of the seasoning. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the kale, and egg
  6. Serve in a basket made of bananna leaves, if possible, and top with coconut cream, kaffir lime and bell pepper
