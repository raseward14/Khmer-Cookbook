Rice Soup with Dried Fish

Ba-Bah Dlrie Ngeeid

Ingredients

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1 part rice with 2 parts water in a rice cooker
  2. Make sure to dry fish in a dehydrator beforehand
  3. let rice cook for 30 minutes
  4. Once Done, add spices:
    • Salt and Pepper
    • Lime juice
    • Bean Sprouts
    • Chili Paste
    • Chives
  5. Serve with a plate of dried/dehydrated fish
