Cooking Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, and add chicken
  2. Rinse the rice.
  3. Sautee 2 garlic cloves for a few seconds in a large sauce pan, add rice, stirring often for 5 minutes. When done throw in with chicken.
  4. After the chicken cooks for about 25 minutes, take out, set aside. When rice has cooked for 15 minutes, add fish sauce, sugar, salt. Continue cooking for another 20 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, chop remaining garlic, sautee it in the oil, then place in a small bowl.
  6. Sautee the mushrooms for a few minutes until tender, then place them in another small bowl.
  7. Place bean sprouts, lime wedges, and scallions in their own bowls.
  8. Shred chicken, then stir into soup.
