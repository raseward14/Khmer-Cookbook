Som La Ga Goh

Ingredients

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dice unripe papaya
  2. Dice bananna
  3. Dice pumpkin
  4. Chop string bean
  5. Slice eggplant
  6. Boil vegetables until soft
  7. Add Turmeric, moringa, and kaffir lime leaves
  8. Optional: Add boiled pork ribs
  9. Roast rice
  10. Add roasted rice and fermented fish Prahok
